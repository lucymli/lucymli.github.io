Lucy M. Li, PhD

I am the Director of Infectious Disease Modeling at The Public Health Company, a California-based biotech startup.

I work at the intersection of technology and infection control, having previous been an academic researcher in this area.

In addition to my work, I want to make the path to leadership easier for the next generation of STEM graduates through mentorship and career guidance, especially for those in under-represented groups.
